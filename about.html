Overview

Smithers Solidarity Spirit Week is open to all those who support striking members in Smither, BC.

Government Workers have been striking for 9 weeks. We ask for a wage that maintains a pace with inflation to avoid continued wage erosion.

During the week of October 27-31, striking BCGEU and PEA members are showing their solidarity through a series of events each day.
Each union member has a chance to participate. Work as a strike line or work individually, there are no restrictions!
