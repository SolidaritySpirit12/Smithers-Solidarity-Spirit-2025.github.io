💪 About Smithers Solidarity Spirit Week 💜

🎟️ Event Poster

🌟 Overview

Smithers Solidarity Spirit Week is a celebration of unity, resilience, and community strength — open to everyone who stands with striking members here in Smithers, BC.

For nine weeks and counting, government workers have held the line, demanding fair wages that keep pace with inflation and protect our families from wage erosion. This week isn’t just about picket signs — it’s about pride, perseverance, and people coming together.

From October 27–31, striking BCGEU and PEA members will showcase their solidarity through a week of fun, creativity, and connection. Each day brings a new activity — from Scrabble showdowns to chili cook-offs — and every member has a chance to take part. Join in as a team or go solo — all that matters is showing your spirit. ✊

💖 Gratitude

A heartfelt thank-you to every single person who continues to stand strong — rain, snow, or shine. Your energy keeps the fires of solidarity burning bright in our community. Together, we’re proving that fair wages and respect at work are worth every step on that line.

☕ Notes

Want to connect? Stop by the line and say hello — conversations are always welcome. And if you bring coffee… well, let’s just say you’ll be extra popular. 😉
