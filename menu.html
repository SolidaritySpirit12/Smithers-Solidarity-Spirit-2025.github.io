🔥 Solidarity Spirits

Every day on the line, our incredible Solidarity Spirits roll up their sleeves and cook from the heart — serving up homemade soups, chili, baking, and comfort food that warms the soul (and the picket hands!).

These volunteers remind us that solidarity isn’t just about standing together — it’s about feeding each other, too. Whether it’s a surprise batch of cookies, a bubbling pot of stew, or a warm cup of cocoa, their care keeps everyone going strong.

If you’ve ever shared a meal on the line, you know — it’s not just food. It’s community. It’s kindness. It’s the fuel that keeps our spirits high through rain, wind, and endless honks of support. 🚗💨

💖 Sponsored By

A heartfelt thank-you to the Smithers Solidarity Cooking-for-the-Line Committee and everyone who’s stirred a pot, baked a loaf, or dropped off a snack this week. You’re the reason our strike stays strong — one delicious bite at a time.

📢 Special Announcements

Hungry to help out? Want to share your favorite dish? Volunteers are always welcome — just chat with your picket captain or the food team to join in. And don’t worry — taste testing totally counts as volunteering. 😉
