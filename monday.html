Solidarity Scrabble - Monday

Event Poster

Game Rules & Details

It’s time to spell out some solidarity! This Monday, October 27, the Bulkley Valley Teachers’ Union (BVTU) is hosting a fun-filled Scrabble Challenge at every picket line in Smithers — open to all striking members, no matter your union.

  • 🏁 A BVTU Executive member will start the game at the beginning of each shift.
  • 🚶‍♀️ Each player walks a lap to collect a Scrabble tile — one tile per lap, seven laps total to collect your seven tiles.
  • 🧩 Once you’ve got your 7th tile, you have 20 minutes to create your best word.
  • 📸 When you're done, text Sarah a photo of your tiles, your word score, your name, and your picket location (details on your check-in table poster!).
  • 💪 Can’t walk laps? No worries — you can team up with someone else and grab your tiles when they finish each lap.
  • 😆 Play fair! No phones, no trading, and no “high-grading” tiles — honour system all the way!

The BVTU will be awarding prizes for:

  • 🏆 Highest total point score
  • 📜 Highest-scoring Latin word
  • ✊ Highest-scoring word related to unions or strikes

Plus — everyone who submits a word will be entered into draw prizes! Winners will receive No Frills gift cards 🎁
